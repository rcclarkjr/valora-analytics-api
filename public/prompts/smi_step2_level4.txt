## The 5 Dimensions (Reference Framework)

As you read each profile, consider these aspects:

1. **Technical Control** - How clean and intentional is the execution?
2. **Compositional Intelligence** - How effectively does the structure serve the work?
3. **Color/Value Architecture** - How sophisticated is the color and value system?
4. **Surface Quality** - How refined or intentional is the physical handling?
5. **Expressive Voice** - How distinctive and emotionally coherent is the work?

## Level 4 Dimensional Profiles

### **4.0 - Just Entered Expert**

- **Technical Control:** Excellent technical execution that confidently serves creative intent; any rule-breaking is clearly deliberate; technique is assured
- **Compositional Intelligence:** Original compositions with strong visual impact; sophisticated use of space, rhythm, and hierarchy; compositions feel authored
- **Color/Value Architecture:** Distinctive color sensibility that's recognizable; uses color expressively and symbolically, not just descriptively; strong palette identity
- **Surface Quality:** Characteristic handling that's part of the aesthetic identity; surface treatment is intentional and consistent; recognizable mark-making
- **Expressive Voice:** Clear, consistent personal style; work is immediately recognizable as by this creator; distinct aesthetic system in place

**Key indicator:** Signature style is established; work has strong personality and creative authority; "you'd recognize other works by this artist"
